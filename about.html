OUR STORY

Inspired by Family. Built with Purpose.

Garcia Harvest Corp began with a simple purpose: to provide healthier, fresher food for our own children.

In a world where many foods contain too many preservatives and chemicals, we wanted our family to have access to better quality food — fresh, honest, and raised with care.

What started as a family commitment became a passion to help other families in the city access fresh eggs, poultry, herbs, and farm products they can feel good about bringing home.
